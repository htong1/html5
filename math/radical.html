Radicals!! :D

A fun and interactive way to find the square roots of non-perfect square numbers.

Let's Begin!

Input the number you want to find the square root of:



Input your guess:



These are the following steps to calculating the closest number to the square root of your inputted number:

Step 1: Divide your number by your guess.

Step 2: Add your guess to that quotient.

Step 3: Divide the sum by 2.

Step 4: Check if the resulting number is the square root of your number. If not, repeat the process and use the number you got as your guess.
